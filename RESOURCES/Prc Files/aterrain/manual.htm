Alien Terrain Manual

Once again, you are to save the World. Moreover, you are the only one who can do it. You will be given with a modern spaceship, lasers, cannons, and other powerful weapons. Your order is to get into the heart of the enemy galaxy. Of course, they are ready to meet you, and to complete the task you have to show them the highest battle mastery, that is why before taking on with a real spaceship you need just bits of training, and this game is going to help you.

Requirements

PalmOS 4.x or above, 160x160 or higher screen resolution, approx. 1.1 Mb of free storage memory

How To Install

HotSync all Palm Application and Palm Database files extracted from the downloaded ZIP archive.

How To Play

Game rules are available under the "Help" menu item from the game itself.
To access the menu, run the game and tap the "Menu" button on the Graffiti area of your PDA.
